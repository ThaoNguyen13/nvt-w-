WELCOME

The Siberian Husky is a medium-sized working
dog, quick and light on his feet and free and
graceful in action. His moderately compact and
well furred body, erect ears and brush tail
suggest his Northern heritage.

His characteristic gait is smooth and seemingly
effortless. He performs his original function in
harness most capably, carrying a light load at a
moderate speed over great distances. His body
proportions and form reflect this basic balance
of power, speed and endurance.
